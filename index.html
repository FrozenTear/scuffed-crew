// The Ethos

Not a server. A clan.

A structured gaming org with game-specific squads and scheduled play nights. Adults only. No drama. Life comes first — the games come second, but we still show up.

Squad structure

Small teams of 5+5 named after in-game lore. Your squad is your crew — the org is the scaffold that holds it together.

Multi-game

Overwatch, Destiny 2, and whatever comes next. The crew spans games — your squad plays one, the org plays them all.

Dedicated voice

Play nights run on TeamSpeak — self-hosted, low latency, built for competitive gaming. Discord handles everything else.

One rule

We're here to play games. No politics, no drama, no soapboxes. Show up, communicate, have fun. Ghost for weeks and your slot goes to someone who gives a damn.
