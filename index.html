// The Ethos

Not a server. A clan.

We're a structured gaming org with game-specific squads, scheduled play nights, and a competitive pipeline through FACEIT. Adults only. Drama-free. Life comes first.

⚔

Squad structure

Small teams of 5+5 named after in-game lore. Your squad is your crew — the org is the scaffold that holds it together.

🏆

Compete on FACEIT

Our teams compete in FACEIT League from Open division up. Real matches, real standings, real path to OWCS qualification.

🎧

Dedicated voice

Play nights run on TeamSpeak — self-hosted, low latency, built for competitive gaming. Discord handles the rest. No process scanning, no telemetry on our voice server.

🛡

One rule

Communicate. Show up when you can, let your team know when you can't. Ghost for weeks and your slot goes to someone who gives a damn.

// Competitive Pipeline

From clan to OWCS

We compete through FACEIT — the official Tier 2 platform for Overwatch esports. No client install, no anti-cheat, no Windows requirement. Just browser + game. Works on Linux.

Our Overwatch teams enter FACEIT League each season starting in Open division. Win enough and you promote to Advanced, Expert, and eventually Master — where OWCS qualification spots are on the line.

TeamSpeak

Voice comms for play nights and scrims. Self-hosted, low latency, trusted by competitive teams for two decades. No process scanning, no behavioral telemetry — just voice on hardware we control.

Added on roster
Why TeamSpeak instead of Discord voice?
Discord voice works fine for casual play — DAVE encryption is legit, audited by Trail of Bits. But for competitive play nights we want dedicated voice: self-hosted, lower latency, no process scanning, no accidental screen-share popups mid-match. TeamSpeak has been the standard for competitive teams for over two decades. It does one thing and does it well.
